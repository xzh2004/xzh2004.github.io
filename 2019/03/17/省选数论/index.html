1.中国剩余定理(CRT)

$ \left\{\begin{matrix}
x\equiv a_1 (mod m_1) \
x\equiv a_2 (mod m_2) \
\vdots \\
x\equiv a_k (mod m_k) \
\end{matrix}\right.$

当$m1,m2,\cdots,mk$为两两互质的整数时，
令$ M=\prod\limits_{i=1}^{k}m_i $
$M_i=M \div m_i $
$t_i \times M_i \times t_i \equiv 1 (mod m_i)$
